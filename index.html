RelSim

Special relativity round-trip simulator (WebGL)
Units: c = 1 (light-years / years)

3D Earth view

A first-person view from an observer on Earth — you see only the light-delayed “ghost ship”, never its true location.

Because light travels at finite speed, the ship appears at its retarded position: where it was when the light you see now left it. Its real position stays invisible; that light hasn’t arrived yet.

Toggle it with the 3D button (top-left) or the 3 key.
